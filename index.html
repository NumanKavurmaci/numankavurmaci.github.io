Job Automation Engine

A workflow engine for job discovery, evaluation, answer preparation, and audit history. It is built around one principle: every automated decision should be inspectable later.

Normalized inputs Explainable scoring Controlled apply flows Reviewable logs
How it works
  1. Inputs are normalized. Scrapers turn page data into stable fields.
  2. Fit is scored. Each job is ranked against the profile with reasons attached.
  3. Answers are prepared. The engine drafts application responses without hiding the source context.
  4. Runs are logged. Decisions, artifacts, and exceptions stay available for review.
